Board Layout

Player 1 is always the first person to join the game and is gifted the white game pieces on the bottom left of the board. Player 2 is gifted the black game pieces on the bottom right of the board.

Game Rules

How to play:

Select either an oval or triangle piece. The piece will be picked up awaiting placement on the Flower of Life. You can tap the selected piece again to drop it back into the cup before placing it. Find an unused slot on the Flower of Life that matches the piece you selected (oval or triangle) and tap it to place.

You can use your turn to place a piece that blocks your opponent from forming a secret symbol.

Winning the game:

The game is over when the board is filled to the point where neither player can form any more symbols.

If you are the player with the higher score total - YOU WIN!

Keep practicing against the computer and other players and, eventually, you'll become a Master of "Da Vinci's Challenge"
